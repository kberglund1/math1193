\begin{document}
\begin{abstract}
    Mathematically describing a colony of bacteria's growth.
\end{abstract}
\maketitle

The number of bacteria in a colony decreases by 5\% per day in certain conditions.

\begin{enumerate}
    \item If the colony starts with 10,000 bacteria, how many are left
    after 1 day in those conditions?
    \item How many are left after 2 days?
    \item 5 days?
    \item When will there be 5000 bacteria left in the colony?
\end{enumerate}

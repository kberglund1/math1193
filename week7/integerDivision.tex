\begin{abstract}
    Division with integers.
\end{abstract}
\maketitle

Find the following without a calculator. Write your answer as a 
mixed number (for example, \(6 \frac{3}{11}\)). Compare your method 
with your group.

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \(16 \div 3\)
    \item \(242 \div 9\)
    \item \(242 \div 19\)
\end{enumerate}

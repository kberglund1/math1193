\title{Factoring a Trinomial}
\author{Kenneth Berglund}

\begin{document}
\begin{abstract}
    Factoring a trinomial.
\end{abstract}
\maketitle

\begin{itemize}
    \item Factor the polynomial \(x^2 - x - 6\). Compare with 
your group and make sense of why each of your methods
works. If you all used the same method, try to find
a different one.
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Solve the equation \(x^2 - x - 6 = 0\).
    \end{itemize}
\end{itemize}

\begin{instructorNotes}
This is meant to draw out students' prior knowledge of factoring and expose them to others'.
\end{instructorNotes}

\begin{abstract}
    Factoring a trinomial.
\end{abstract}
\maketitle

Factor the polynomial \(x^2 - x - 6\). Compare with 
your group and make sense of why each of your methods
works. If you all used the same method, try to find
a different one.

\begin{itemize}
    Solve the equation \(x^2 - x - 6\).
\end{itemize}

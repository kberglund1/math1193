\begin{abstract}
    Helping Jaden evaluate an expression.
\end{abstract}
\maketitle

Jaden was given the following problem:

\textit{Let \(f(x) = x^2 + x + 1\). Find \(f(a + 2)\).}

Jaden's work is as follows:

\begin{align*}
    f(a + 2) & = a + 2^2 + a + 2 + 1 \\
    & = a + 4 + a + 3 \\
    & = 2a + 7
\end{align*}

Is Jaden's work correct? Why or why not? Where did he 
go wrong? What did he do correctly?

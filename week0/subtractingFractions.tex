\title{Subtracting Fractions}
\author{Kenneth Berglund}

\begin{document}
\begin{abstract}
    Refreshing the process of subtracting fractions.
\end{abstract}
\maketitle

Subtract: \(\frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{3}\). You answer should be a fraction.

Explain your process to a friend. If you have different processes,
try to compare and contrast them. 

Together, come up with an answer to \(\frac{2}{7} - \frac{8}{9}\).
